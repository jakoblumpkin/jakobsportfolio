What is a web developer?

A web developer is someone who build and structure websites. Rather its the design of a website, the functionality or the features. Developers uses a variety of different tools to get the job done. That could be coding languages, frameworks or templates. Some examples of coding languages are python, javascript, php or html\css and the list goes on. In conclusion, that's the basics of what a web developer is.

How i got into web development?

It all started with me preparing to transition from the Air Force to being a civilian, after four years of service. Personally, as i started to initiate a new chapter of life, i wanted a occupation/career i can be proud of. So i began to research different industries and options i have as a recent separated veteran. I proceeded to scroll through the internet and I came upon a couple of self-taught web developers on youtube and it gave me a gut feeling inside me that this is for me. I started doing my research on everything that has to do with this career field. Then i proceeded to buy online courses and watch plenty of tutorials, so i can learn the basics needed to build projects. While on this journey of knowledge seeking, I would play around trying to build different types of projects. I would practice on building algorithms using python on codewars.com and ranking up on the leaderboards. To make a long story short, that's how i got started with web development. This industry grants me the opportunity to make money and express my creativity.
